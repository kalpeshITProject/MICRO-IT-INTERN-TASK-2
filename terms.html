Terms and conditions are the rules and guidelines for using a service or product, essentially a legally binding agreement between a business and its users. They define acceptable use, limitations, and responsibilities for both parties, ensuring clarity and protection for all involved.  Here's a more detailed breakdown:

Legally Binding:

Terms and conditions are contracts outlining what users can and cannot do when using a service, product, or website.

Framework of Expectations

They establish a framework for the relationship between the business and its users, covering topics like acceptable use, intellectual property, and dispute resolution.

Business Protection:

They help businesses protect themselves in case of legal disputes by outlining user behavior expectations and limitations.

User Understanding:

They also help users understand their rights and obligations when using a service or product.
